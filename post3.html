Global Ocean Cleanup Initiative

July 1, 2025 • Echoes Of The Deep

  1. Home
  2. Posts
  3. Global Ocean Cleanup Initiative

Our Global Ocean Cleanup Initiative connects coastal communities, schools, and private partners to remove plastic at the most effective choke points: river mouths, harbors, and tourist beaches. With standardized data collection via our mobile app, every cleanup logs material types and weights, helping governments identify priority sources and improve waste management.

Beyond cleanups, the initiative tackles root causes. We’re piloting deposit-return schemes with local retailers, supporting fishermen to retrieve ghost nets, and funding small enterprises that upcycle collected plastics into useful products. Quarterly dashboards will publish progress, hotspot maps, and policy recommendations so cities can act on evidence, not guesswork.
