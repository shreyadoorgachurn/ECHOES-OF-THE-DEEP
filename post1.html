New Marine Protected Areas Established

June 12, 2025 • Echoes Of The Deep

  1. Home
  2. Posts
  3. New Marine Protected Areas Established

Three new Marine Protected Areas (MPAs) have been designated across priority habitats in the Pacific, covering spawning reefs, mangrove nurseries, and major migratory highways. The new status restricts destructive extraction while allowing low-impact activities that sustain local livelihoods. Together, these MPAs form an ecological network designed to boost biodiversity, rebuild fish stocks, and improve coastal resilience to climate-driven storms.

Our team will support co-management with communities through ranger trainings, participatory mapping, and long-term monitoring using acoustic sensors and reef health surveys. Over the next 12 months we will publish quarterly updates on compliance, habitat recovery, and socio-economic benefits, ensuring the MPAs deliver measurable conservation outcomes that people can see and feel.
